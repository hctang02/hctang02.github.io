About Me

I am Haocheng Tang, a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at Peking University, advised by Prof. Wen Gao and Prof. Siwei Ma.

I received my B.Eng. degree in Computer Science from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2024, and I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. degree at Peking University from 2024 to 2029.

My research interests lie in 3D Digital Humans, with a particular focus on efficient representation and compression for digital humans, human-object and human-environment interaction, and digital human applications for sports scenarios.

I am also actively involved in research on AVS standards for digital human representation and coding, and have led the establishment of the VRU AhG Digital Human Group to advance the standardization of digital humans.
